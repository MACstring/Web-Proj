About Us

Established in 2010 in Islamabad, Pakistan, 4U Trade and Serve (4U T&S) swiftly emerged as a leading trading firm.
Its enduring success stems from a culmination of factors, notably the team's collective 22+ years of experience and the unwavering trust bestowed upon 4U T&S by its valued customers, dedicated employees, and esteemed partners. Notably, the company proudly represents globally acclaimed names such as HP, Dell, Acer, APC, Microsoft, EdiMax, D-Link, Linksys, Cisco, TP-Link, among others. Distinguished as a significant contributor to national economic growth, 4U T&S actively engages in fostering development. Motivated teams across its business, service divisions, and subsidiary companies diligently cater to customers' needs, aligning with both short-term demands and long-term objectives. Since its inception, 4U T&S and its associated entities have played a pivotal role in elevating productivity in the realms of information technology (Hardware, LAN & Software) and electrical solutions, while seamlessly sourcing products from the global market.
